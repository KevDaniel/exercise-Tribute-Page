Cristiano Ronaldo "El Bicho"

The man that changed the soccer and has scored more goals in history

Here´s a timeline of Cristiano Ronaldo´s carrer

Formation and childhood (1985 - 2002)

  • 1985 (February 5th): Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro was born on Funchal, Madeira, Portugal.
  • 1997: At the age of 12, Cristiano leaves Madeira to join Sporting of Lisboa, where his talent helps him to get attention of their coach

Sporting of Lisboa (2002 - 2003)

  • 2002: Cristiano´s debut with the first team of Sporting of Lisboa at the age of 17. We will show his hability, velocity and technique.
  • 2003 (Friend game against Manchester United): Plays an incredible game against Manchester City, which makes Sir Alex Ferguson being interested on "El Bicho"

Manchester United (2003 - 2009)

  • 2003 (August): Cristiano signs with Manchester United for around £12 million, becoming the most expensive teenager in English football history.
  • 2007-08: His breakout season, winning the Premier League and the Champions League. Cristiano scores 42 goals in all competitions and wins his first Ballon d'Or in 2008.
  • 2009: After six successful years, he wins three Premier League titles, one FA Cup, two League Cups, and one Champions League.

Real Madrid (2009 - 2018)

  • 2009: Cristiano joins Real Madrid for a record €94 million fee, marking the start of a new era at the club.
  • 2011-12: Helps Real Madrid win La Liga, and during his time at the club, he becomes the all-time top scorer.
  • 2013: Wins his second Ballon d'Or.
  • 2014: Helps Real Madrid win their 10th Champions League title ("La Décima").
  • 2016: Leads Real Madrid to their 11th Champions League title and wins the Euro 2016 with Portugal, his first major international title.
  • 2017: Cristiano wins his fifth Ballon d'Or and leads the team to their 12th Champions League title.
  • 2018: Ends his Real Madrid career with four Champions League titles (2014, 2016, 2017, 2018) and as the club’s all-time top scorer with over 450 goals.

Juventus (2018 - 2021)

  • 2018: Cristiano signs with Juventus for €100 million.
  • 2018-19: Wins the Serie A title and is pivotal for the team, although they don’t win the Champions League during his stint.
  • 2020: Helps secure another Serie A title and remains the team's top scorer in both seasons.
